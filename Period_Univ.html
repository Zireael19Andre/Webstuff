Tianjin



University

Hard works always pays off. After senior, I was admitted to Hebei University of Technology, a first rank university in China. Although, college like an isolated island in the ocean, if you keep stay in the comfort zone, you will sink faster than you think.

So I try to Maintaining the momentum, and start up a Study Abroad Program. Besides keep high GPA around 3.2, I also start to self-learning Japanese since sophomore year, as well as TOEIC. After received JLPT N2 Certificate and TOEIC Score Certificate in the junior year, I sent my first international email in my life to professor and been admitted for a further education chance.

Life is made up of a series of choices. All my missing times with After-school activities become the step to my dream come true.
